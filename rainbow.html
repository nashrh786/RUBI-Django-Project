RAINBOW MEAL

"Eat the Rainbow, Nourish Your Body."

Detailed Diet Plan for Rainbow Meals:


1. Red (Tomatoes, Strawberries, Red Peppers, Apples, Cherries)
Key Nutrients: Lycopene, Vitamin C, Fiber, Antioxidants
Health Benefits: Helps prevent heart disease, improves skin health, and boosts the immune system.
Recipe Ideas:
Red Pepper and Tomato Soup Strawberry Spinach Salad Apple and Cinnamon Oatmeal


2. Orange (Carrots, Sweet Potatoes, Oranges, Peaches, Mangoes) Key Nutrients: Beta-carotene, Vitamin C, Fiber Health Benefits: Promotes good vision, supports the immune system, and maintains healthy skin. Recipe Ideas: Roasted Sweet Potatoes with Cinnamon Carrot and Ginger Soup Mango and Avocado Smoothie


3. Yellow (Bananas, Corn, Yellow Peppers, Pineapples) Key Nutrients: Vitamin C, Potassium, Folate Health Benefits: Boosts mood, supports heart health, and aids digestion. Recipe Ideas: Pineapple and Coconut Rice Banana Smoothie with Almond Butter Yellow Bell Pepper and Corn Stir-Fry


4. Green (Spinach, Kale, Broccoli, Cucumbers, Zucchini) Key Nutrients: Vitamin K, Folate, Iron, Calcium Health Benefits: Supports strong bones, improves digestion, and helps detoxify the body. Recipe Ideas: Green Smoothie (Spinach, Banana, Almond Milk) Kale and Quinoa Salad Broccoli and Zucchini Stir-Fry


5. Blue/Purple (Blueberries, Eggplant, Purple Grapes, Plums, Purple Cabbage) Key Nutrients: Anthocyanins, Vitamin C, Fiber, Potassium Health Benefits: Improves brain function, reduces inflammation, and promotes heart health. Recipe Ideas: Blueberry and Almond Yogurt Parfait Eggplant Parmesan Purple Cabbage Slaw with Lemon Vinaigrette
